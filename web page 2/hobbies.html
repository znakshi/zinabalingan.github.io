Zina's Hobbies and Interests

Hobbies

  • Reading: I like to read fiction, escpecially genres like fantasy and thriller. I like stories that have interesting plot and character.
  • Photography: Capturing moments with my camera is fun. I like collecting the memories I have with my family and friends. I also like to take photos of sunsets, clouds and nature.
  • Gaming: Playing games are one of my favorite hobbies, especially playing with friends. I like games that have unique gameplay and interesting characters.
  • Drawing: I draw either on paper or digitally. I like to draw characters I like from animated shows and video games.

Interests

Besides my hobbies, I have a variety of other interests:

  • Music: Listening to music always lifts up my mood. I like to discover new artists and even listen to old ones.
  • Cafes: Going to new and old cafes. Especially with friends, we find the ones with good coffee and food.
  • Instruments: I like them but I can only do basics on some instruments. I would love to find an instrument that I can excel in.
